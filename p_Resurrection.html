Resurrection
Saleginella was known as a key ingredient in the resurrection ritual performed by Taoist priests. It was known as the resurrection plant due to its unique property of curling and shriveling up into a bud-like shape when dried and uncurling, spreading healthy green leaves when accumulating enough moisture. So what if these plants can actually resurrect cadavers? What if they can take control of cadavers like a parasite taking control of its host? It would be a sight to see corpses being reanimated.

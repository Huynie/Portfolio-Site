Pronged アヒル
The Goal for this was to design a vehicle that operates on both land and water. So immediately I thought about amphibious animals and their forms. So the first two picks were an alligator and a duck. The last one was a pivot spitball idea of land, water, and air. Although a stingray isn’t an amphibious animal, its form makes for great aerodynamic deisign and purposes, so I gave that a try in my priliminary exploration and sketching phase. I ended up going with the duck form for its less aggresive and balanced form.
